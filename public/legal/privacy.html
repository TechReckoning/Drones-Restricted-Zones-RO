3. Why we process it, and our legal basis

  • Providing accounts and saved history — performance of our contract with you (Art. 6(1)(b)).
  • Processing subscriptions and payments — performance of the contract (Art. 6(1)(b)) and compliance with accounting/tax obligations (Art. 6(1)(c)).
  • Security, fraud prevention and service reliability — our legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f)).

4. Processors and third parties

We share data only with service providers acting on our behalf under data-processing agreements:

  • Supabase — authentication, database and hosting for your account and saved zones (hosted within the European Union).
  • Stripe — payment processing and subscription management. Stripe is the processor of your payment data as an independent controller for its own compliance purposes; see stripe.com/privacy.
  • OpenStreetMap — map tiles are loaded from OpenStreetMap servers, which necessarily receive your IP address to serve the imagery.

5. International transfers

Some processors (e.g. Stripe) may process data outside the European Economic Area. Where they do, transfers are protected by adequacy decisions or the European Commission's Standard Contractual Clauses.

6. Retention

Account and saved-zone data are kept for as long as your account is active. You may delete saved zones at any time and request deletion of your account. Billing and invoicing records are retained for the period required by Romanian fiscal law (10 years). Server logs are kept for a limited period for security purposes.

7. Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the right to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and to object to processing, as well as to withdraw consent where processing is based on it. To exercise any right, contact us at office@zorile-energie.com.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Romanian supervisory authority, the Autoritatea Națională de Supraveghere a Prelucrării Datelor cu Caracter Personal (ANSPDCP) — dataprotection.ro.

8. Cookies and local storage

The Service uses only strictly necessary cookies and browser storage. See our Cookie Policy for details.

9. Changes

We may update this policy; the "last updated" date above reflects the current version. Material changes will be notified in the app.

Cookie Policy

Last updated: 14 July 2026

This policy explains the cookies and similar browser-storage technologies used by Drones Restricted Zones RO, operated by Zorile Energie S.R.L.

We use only strictly necessary cookies and local storage — the technologies required to sign you in and to take payments. We do not use analytics, advertising or cross-site tracking cookies. Because strictly necessary technologies are exempt from prior-consent requirements under the ePrivacy Directive, we do not ask you to consent to them; we simply inform you here.

Managing cookies

You can delete or block cookies and clear local storage via your browser settings. Note that blocking strictly necessary storage will prevent you from signing in or completing a subscription. Because we do not use non-essential cookies, there is nothing further to opt out of.

Changes

If we introduce non-essential cookies in the future, we will update this policy and request your prior consent through a cookie banner before setting them.

2. The service

Drones Restricted Zones RO is a digital tool that displays UAV restricted zones in Romania (data sourced from ROMATSA) and lets subscribers draw a planned flying zone, analyse overlaps with restricted areas, export it as KML and save it to their account. Viewing the map is free; the "Pro" features are available on a free trial and then by paid subscription.

⚠️ The Service is an unofficial aid. It does not grant flight authorisations. Always verify against official ROMATSA sources and current NOTAMs, and comply with all applicable aviation rules, before flying.
